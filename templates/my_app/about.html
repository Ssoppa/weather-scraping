The Idea

Have you ever wanted to know just what's the weather look like today and got a lot of unnecessary information?

Well, I have. That's why I got this idea, show just the meaningful data.

Inspiration

Everything started when I decided that I would learn about Web Scraping.

Since I google "How is the weather" a lot, and the websites are too visually stressful, that's what I was going to stackle.

Creative Research

I made a research about what really matters about weather and asked people about how they search it. With that in mind, I finally was able to design this website.

Development

After I learn about WebScraping, I choose a website to scrape, but as the BeautifulSoup couldn't get all the data from there, I changed it to another one.

Finally, learned Materialize by doing the frontend.
